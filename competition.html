2025 Season

Competition & Schedule

From our first invitational to the state championship — here's how our season unfolds.

📅   Specific dates and locations will be posted here once confirmed. Check back soon or follow our socials for updates.
🏁

STEP 01

Invitationals

Invitationals are unofficial warm-up tournaments hosted by schools or organizations before the official season begins. They're a great opportunity for our members to experience the competition format, test their preparation, and get comfortable under test conditions — all in a lower-stakes environment. DNSO participates in invitationals to sharpen skills and identify areas to focus on before regionals.

Season Opener
📍

STEP 02

Regionals

The Regional tournament is the first official leg of the Science Olympiad season. Teams compete across all events and are ranked against other schools in our region. Top-placing teams earn the right to advance to the State Championship. Regionals are where the real season begins — preparation, execution, and teamwork all come together for a full-day competition.

Official Season
🏆

STEP 03

State Championship

The State Championship brings together the top Science Olympiad teams from across California to compete for the title. Qualifying for State is a major achievement, and competing at this level represents the highest point of our season. DNSO trains all year with State in mind — every practice, every build day, and every study session is preparation for this moment.
