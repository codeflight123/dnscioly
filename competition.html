Season Timeline

Dec 1 – Dec 6, 2025

Boyceville Satellite Invitational

Our season opener — a massive online invitational run by Boyceville with 300 teams nationwide. A great first look at the competition landscape.

🏁 Invitational · Online

Jan 17, 2026

USC Invitational

One of the largest invitationals in the nation with 91 Div. C teams. Del Norte "Moto Moto" placed 11th out of 91 teams at USC.

🏁 Invitational · 11th / 91

Feb 21, 2026

Triton Invitational (UCSD)

Hosted at UC San Diego, our final warm-up before Regionals. Del Norte A placed 5th out of 41 teams — a strong performance heading into the official season.

🏁 Invitational · 5th / 41

Feb 28, 2026

San Diego Regional Championship

The official qualifying tournament held at University of San Diego. Del Norte "Moto Moto" finished 2nd out of 62 teams, punching our ticket to the State Championship!

📍 Regionals · 2nd / 62 ✧ State Qualifier

Apr 11, 2026

🏆 SoCal State Championship @ Caltech

The pinnacle of our season — held at California Institute of Technology with 32 of the top teams in Southern California. Del Norte placed 7th out of 32 teams. An incredible finish to the year.

🏆 State Championship · 7th / 32
